FAQ

Openmeme â€“ Community Economy

Openmeme operates as a 'community economy'. Users who help develop the platform by completing tasks receive Tokens representing the platform.

Profit Sharing with Openmeme

Openmeme is a business that earns from advertising and an NFT marketplace. All profits are paid out to token holders. 'Staking' your coins allows you to receive profits in other tokens that can be immediately monetized.

Development of the platform

The platform grows by creating content by users, which attracts others and spreads across the internet. The more and better the content, the more users on Openmeme. As they say: "Content is king."

NFT technology and memes

On Openmeme, memes are created from the NFT elements of other users. You can add your own element, template, sticker, anything you want, to the collection. If your creation becomes popular and is used by others, it will increase in value. In the final version of the platform, you will receive tokens for others using your elements.

Validation

All elements undergo independent validation. Validators - selected users - receive compensation in coins for good validation. For mistakes, they can lose this right. 70% of "yes" votes allows an element to be minted on the blockchain.

Open collections

Everyone can create their own open collection and earn from its popularity:

  • 1% from the earnings of users adding elements to your collection.
  • 10% of the value when someone sells an element from your collection.
  • 10% of the value of the reward for a quest associated with your collection.

Tasks & Rewards

Tokens can be earned for:

  • Adding elements to the collection and acceptance by validators.
  • Trading elements with other users.
  • Your meme on the main page.
  • Your element being used X times by other users, appearing on the main page.

Summary

This is a new type of platform, where the community is the owner and co-creator of its success. Everything is based on blockchain technology.

Current status

The platform is in beta version, available only for desktop users. It does not work on mobile devices.
